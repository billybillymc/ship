# web/src/components/week/

Sprint/week detail and visualization components.

## Files

- **WeekDetailView.tsx** -- Three-column sprint detail layout: header with progress bar, left column with burndown graph and standup feed, right column with issues list.
- **WeekProgressGraph.tsx** -- SVG burndown chart visualizing sprint progress (completed hours vs. scope) against a target pace line.
- **WeekTimeline.tsx** -- Horizontally scrolling infinite-scroll week timeline with progress indicators, month headers, and "Today" marker.
- **index.ts** -- Barrel exports for week components.
